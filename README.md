## Legal & ethical notes

All demonstrations in the deck use synthetic / placeholder targets. Live demonstrations against any real subject must be pre-authorised, must follow the relevant Pakistani statutory framework — **NAO 1999**, **AMLA 2010**, **PECA 2016**, **Investigation Act 2022**, **Qanun-e-Shahadat 1984** — and must be logged in the case file.

OSINT is corroboration, not conclusion. Every finding must be second-sourced before it goes into a court submission.
